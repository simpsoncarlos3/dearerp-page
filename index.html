FAQ

Questions you might have.

A short FAQ to answer the basics. We’re happy to go deeper on a call.

Is DearERP a replacement for Odoo?
No. DearERP is an AI layer on top of your existing Odoo instance. Your data stays in Odoo — we simply make it dramatically easier to query, configure and use.
Which Odoo versions do you support?
We focus on modern, actively used Odoo versions and typical deployment patterns (on-prem, cloud or Odoo.sh). Share your current setup in a call and we’ll confirm compatibility.
How do you handle security & permissions?
DearERP respects Odoo access rights. Users can only see and act on the data their Odoo profile allows. You can also restrict which actions the AI is allowed to perform directly vs. only suggest.
Can you customize DearERP for our industry?
Yes. We can tune prompts, workflows and “quick actions” for verticals like trading, manufacturing, services, distribution and SaaS. The more context you share, the more tailored DearERP becomes.
